Meet the Creators



Daniel Johnson

Daniel was born into a humble Slavic family. Although never living in Eastern Europe, he is very proud of his heritage. He started wearing tracksuits regularly at the age of 6 and never looked back. His life changed when he met his future business partner, Dylan Zapzalka. This short but fierce man and Daniel started making statistical interfaces on a Ti-84 calculator while bored in STAT 368 class. Daniel's story is one of the American dream. He proved that through hard work anything is possible, especially in a track suit.




Dylan Zapzalka

Dylan Zapzalka was born atop of the sacred mountain Mount Paektu as a new star appeared in the sky. Things were looking bright for Dylan's future, until an unfortunate accident of inheriting his parents genes left him at 5'7, a height that would be the cause for his irremediable Napoleon Complex. Despite his short comings, he was able to start a successful business with his business partner, Daniel Johnson. For his hobbies, he likes to enjoy his time driving around a pretentious cherry red sports car to every ones dismay.
